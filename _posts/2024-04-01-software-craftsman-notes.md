## Professionalism
{: #professionalism }

Professionalism is not only about delivery. It is also about honesty, feedback,
maintainability, and the willingness to improve the system after it ships.

## Practice
{: #practice }

Deliberate practice keeps skills sharp. Reading, pairing, refactoring exercises,
and side projects all create space to improve without production pressure.

## Career
{: #career }

Career ownership means choosing environments where learning and quality are
possible. The craft grows through both technical work and professional judgment.
