## Shaping
{: #shaping }

Shaping is the work before execution. The goal is to make a problem concrete
enough for a team to build, while still leaving space for design and technical
judgment.

## Appetite
{: #appetite }

An appetite is a budget for attention. Instead of estimating how long a fixed
scope will take, Shape Up asks how much time the problem deserves.

## Betting
{: #betting }

The betting table keeps projects intentional. A team chooses a small number of
promising bets and gives them enough uninterrupted time to finish.
