## Habit
{: #habit }

Small notes are useful when they stay close to the decisions that created them.
Capture the context, the tradeoff, and the result before the details fade.

## Shape
{: #shape }

A good engineering note is brief, searchable, and specific enough to help the
next reader avoid repeating the same investigation.
